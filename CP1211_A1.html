Home

My favorite pass time/ hobby is gaming. I enjoy playing video games of all different types, from horror games to puzzle games and PC games to console games. I have been playing video games since I was a little girl with my Game Boy Color. If I remember correctly the first game I ever played was a Pokemon game that I borrowed from one of my older cousins.

Gaming Community

Not only do I enjoy playing video games myself, but I also enjoy watching others play. Watching videos on YouTube, live streams on Twitch, or just sitting next to friends. I have always found it entertaining to watch other people play the games that I love playing myself. It also helps that when on these sites it makes the viewer feel like they are part of a community. You get to interact with people that have the same interests as you. From the content creators to the other people watching the gameplay, these websites connect people who enjoy playing video games and those who have never played before. You get to interact with like-minded people, while also learning new things about games and gaming that you may have never known.


Gaming Consoles

The three main companies that have been making consoles for years are Microsoft, Sony, and Nintendo. In recent years Microsoft and Sony have been head to head in what people have dubbed the "console wars". Microsoft Xbox vs Sony Playstation, while Nintendo is still making consoles they have started to focus more on handhelds with the Nintendo Switch. Even if some people like to argue about which company is better I really don't have a favorite. I enjoy and own many consoles from all three companies.


Even if I consider myself more of a PC gamer I do have a fairly large collection of gaming consoles. I have made it a goal of mine to try and collect all of the different generations of consoles from each company. Plus no matter how much I play on my PC I always get struck with nostalgia when I hear the starting sound of the Nintendo Gamecube starting up.
