Favorite Games

I have played many games in the past. Spent hundreds of hours playing different ones, and sometimes hundreds of hours on only one. My Steam library alone is filled with over 200 games. Though I haven't had the chance to play all of them yet, out of the games that I have played and completed these are some of my favorites:


  • Don't Starve
  • Oxenfree
  • Hollow Knight
  • Fallout 4
  • Resident Evil 8

I have many more games that I love and would put in the list above but then the list might start to grow a little too long. I can never seem to settle on one game alone that I can call my favorite, at least not for a long period of time. My answer could change depending on what mood I am in or what game I have been playing recently. Honestly, I'm sure my answer would change depending on the time of day you asked me. The games I listed above are just ones that stood out to me in my steam library, if I went through my console games the list may end up being completely different again. And to ask me to pick out one singular game to call my favorite out of any that I have played would be an impossible task.
