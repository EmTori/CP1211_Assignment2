Gaming Community

Not only do I enjoy playing video games myself, but I also enjoy watching others play. Watching videos on YouTube, live streams on Twitch, or just sitting next to friends. I have always found it entertaining to watch other people play the games that I love playing myself.


These sites make the viewer feel like they are part of a community. You get to interact with people that have the same interests as you. From the content creators to the other people watching the gameplay, these websites connect people who enjoy playing video games. You get to interact with like-minded people and enjoy you hobbies together.
